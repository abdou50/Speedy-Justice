CRIMINAL LAWS

1) Domestic-violence: Women and girls are protected by the Law on Eliminating Violence againstWomen, No. 58 of 2017

2) Abortion for rape survivors: Abortion has been decriminalized.

3) Rape (other than of a spouse): Article 227 of the Penal Code criminalizes rape, which is defined as any act of intercourse with a man or woman without consent, regardless of the nature or the method used.

4) Marital rape: Marital rape is not specifically criminalized. The Personal Status Code requires both spouses to “fulfil their marital obligations according to custom.”

5) Sexual harassment: Article 226 of the Penal Code prohibits sexual harassment.

6) Human trafficking: Law No. 61 of 2016 on Preventing and Combating Trafficking in Persons provides comprehensive measures against trafficking.

7) Adultery: Adultery is an offence under Article 236 of the Penal Code.
