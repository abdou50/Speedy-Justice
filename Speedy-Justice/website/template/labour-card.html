LABOUR LAWS

1) Right to equal pay for the same work as men: Women have the right to equal pay for the same work as men. Article 5 bis of the Labour Code prohibits discrimination between men and women in the application of the Code.

2) Dismissal for pregnancy: Article 20 of the Labour Code prohibits employers from dismissing a worker because of her pregnancy.

3) Domestic workers: Domestic workers do not benefit from the protective provisions of the Labour Code.

4) Paid maternity leave: Article 64 of the Labour Code states that women are entitled to 30 days of maternity leave. This s below the ILO standard of 14 weeks.

5) Legal restrictions on women's work: he Labour Code includes legal restrictions on women's employ-ment in undertaking night work, mining, and scrap metal work.
